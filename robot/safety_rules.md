# Robot Safety Rules

- Confirm the emergency stop is accessible, tested, and understood by all operators before robot power is enabled.
- Keep the workspace clear of unnecessary tools, cables, and people before running movement or strategy tests.
- Handle batteries carefully by checking charge level, inspecting for damage, and using approved charging procedures.
- Test new behaviors at low speed or in simulation before attempting full operation on hardware.
- Stop testing immediately if the robot behaves unexpectedly or any sensor, actuator, or communication link becomes unreliable.
