# Robot Startup Checklist

Complete the following checks before operating the robot:

1. Inspect the work area and remove any unnecessary objects from the robot's operating zone.
2. Verify that the battery level or external power supply is sufficient and correctly connected.
3. Check that all mechanical parts, cables, and sensor connections are secure and undamaged.
4. Confirm that the network connection is available when remote monitoring or control is required.
5. Test the emergency-stop button and confirm that it can be reached easily by the operator.
6. Power on the robot and verify that the controller starts without warning or fault messages.
7. Confirm that the required sensors, actuators, and communication modules are detected.
8. Run a low-speed functional test before starting the planned task.
9. Record any faults, unusual sounds, or failed checks before continuing.
