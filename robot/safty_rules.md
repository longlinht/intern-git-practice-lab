# Robot Safety Rules

- Verify that the emergency stop button is functional and accessible before operating the robot.
- Maintain a safe distance from the robot during startup, motion testing, walking, and kicking actions.
- Ensure that the testing area is clear of people, cables, tools, and other obstacles before running any robot command.
- Do not operate the robot without supervision. A team member should always monitor the robot during testing.
- Stop the test immediately if the robot shows unstable movement, loses balance, or moves outside the assigned testing area.
- Avoid touching or forcing the robot joints while the motors are powered or the robot is in a stiff mode.
- Report any abnormal behavior, hardware issue, or network problem before continuing the test.
